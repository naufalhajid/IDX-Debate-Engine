  Run each check below. Skip any check where required data is absent -- DO NOT fabricate.

  IF Altman Z-Score > 10 AND Debt/Equity > 1.0:
    -> Flag: "Z-Score vs DER Inconsistency -- cross-check with interest coverage."

  IF no inconsistencies detected -> write: "No red flags detected."
